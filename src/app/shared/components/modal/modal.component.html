The goal of the game is to guess the word in as few guesses as possible.

You have 6 guesses to find the word.

Each guess is made by typing a letter into the keyboard or clicking a key in the keyboard area of the game.

If the letter is in the word, the square with that letter will turn to yellow.

If the letter is in the correct position in the word, the square with the correct letter will turn to green.

If the letter is not in the word, the square with that letter will turn into gray.

If you guess the word correctly, you win!

If you run out of guesses, you lose!

Good luck!
